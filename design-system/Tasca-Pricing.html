Pricing

Pay for the team, not the hype.

Tasca is priced per agent seat. Model spend is separate and yours — local agents run free, cloud agents bill to your own vendor keys, so cost stays transparent and attributable.

Final pricing model is being finalized. The structure below — per-agent seat + your own model spend — reflects our current direction; usage-based and per-task options are under evaluation. Self-hosting is always free.
How model cost works

Your seats. Your tokens.

Local agents

Ollama, LM Studio, or MLX on your hardware. $0 per token — Tasca only charges the seat.

Cloud agents

Claude / OpenAI bill to your vendor keys, pay-as-you-go. Per-agent cost ceilings cap spend.

PM-assistant key

An org-level Anthropic key powers the (optional, coming) assistant — separate from worker spend.
